Conclusions


  • With K-Means and PCA , we can reduce the time for computing the top 10 matches.
  • In terms of Speed of computation , performing PCA is the fastest out of the three approaches with K-means atleast taking 5 times more time than PCA and linear comparison taking atleast 7 times more time than PCA
  • In terms of Accuracy , K-Means provides slightly better accuracy with the results we got compared to PCA.With PCA, we can achieve a minimum of 60% accuracy and with K-means we acheive a minimum of 70% accuracy
  • Can combine K-means and PCA for compare and verify its performance and accuracy as a future step

Note:- the above conclusion are based on running multiple test cases and inputs on the dataset
